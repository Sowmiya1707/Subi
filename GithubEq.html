Popular Tourist Places

Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of various materials that stretch across northern China. It is one of the longest structures ever built by humans.

Grand Canyon, USA

The Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in the state of Arizona, USA. It is known for its dramatic geological formations and breathtaking views.
